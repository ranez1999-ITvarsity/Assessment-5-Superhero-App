Black Widow

Bio

Born Natalia Alianova Romanoff On November 22, 1984, she was taken into the KGB, where she was trained in the Red Room, a Soviet training program to create brainwashed assassins. Natalia was exceptionally gifted in the arts of spy-craft and soon became one of the most ruthless students there. With her reputation, she became the master assassin the Black Widow.
